PocketPiglet 1.2.1

Developer: Oleg Derevenetz <oleg.derevenetz@gmail.com>

PocketPiglet is a cute 3D animated piglet who lives inside your phone. Play mini-games with him to keep him happy. Watch several funny animations with your little friend in the title role.

If you have any questions regarding this application, you can contact application developer on this website: http://pocketpiglet.sourceforge.net/.

Mini-games

Feed the Piglet

Your piglet is hungry! Remember sandwich recipe and add the ingredients in the right order to make the perfect sandwich for your favorite pet. Enjoy funny animation when the game is completed.

Wash the Piglet

Your piglet wants to take a bath and play with soap bubbles! Help him to catch and pop as many bubbles as you can. Enjoy funny animation when the game is completed.

Piglet Puzzle

Your piglet is trying to solve a puzzle! Help him to solve a sliding puzzle as fast as you can.

Interactive animations

Make your piglet laugh

Stroke your piglet's tummy to make him laugh.

Piglet's vertigo

Shake your phone to to get your piglet to fall due to vertigo.

Exploding cake

Make fun of your pet by giving him an exploding cake.

Sweet candy

Make your little pet happy by giving him a sweet treat.
