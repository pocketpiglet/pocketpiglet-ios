PocketPiglet 1.2.0

Developer: Natalia Derevenets <nna911@inbox.ru>

PocketPiglet is a cute 3D animated piglet who lives inside your phone. Talk with piglet, and he will repeat everything you say with a funny voice. Play mini-games with him to keep him happy. Watch several funny animations with your little friend in the title role.

If you have any questions regarding this application, you can contact application developer on this website: http://pocketpiglet.sourceforge.net/.
